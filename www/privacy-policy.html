Privacy policy

The Aspen Leadership Group takes the protection of privacy very seriously. Personal data will be used to provide service to you but will not be shared with or sold to third parties without your permission. Please note that our website includes links to third-party websites, whose privacy policies we do not control.

Candidates: We consider the search process to be confidential. Information you share with us, including the fact that you are a candidate, will only be shared with your permission.

Consent: By using this website or otherwise submitting personal information, you expressly consent to the terms in this statement.

Changes to Privacy Policy: Any updates to this policy will be posted on the website (News section) at the time of the change.

Questions: If you have questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please contact us.
